### Step 3: Customize Your Settings ‚öôÔ∏è

**Notification Timing:**
- Set how many days before expiry you want alerts (default: 3 days)
- Example: Set to "5" to get alerts 5 days before items expire

**Quiet Hours:**
- Set times when you DON'T want notifications
- Example: 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM for peaceful sleep

**Notification Frequency:**
- Choose 1-4 times per day
- 1 = 9 AM only
- 2 = 9 AM and 6 PM
- 3 = 9 AM, 2 PM, and 7 PM
- 4 = 8 AM, 12 PM, 4 PM, and 8 PM
